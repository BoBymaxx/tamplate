TreeManager

Choose an option that fits your needs best

Send by email

Send fully customized emails linked to trees.

  • Upload your details like company logo
  • Select or upload an email header
  • Send emails one by one or in bulk


Download spreadsheet

Get all trees’ links in a single spreadsheet.

  • Upload your details like company logo
  • Select the number of trees’ links you need
  • Download an Excel with links for further usage


Download QR Code

Get links to your trees in the form of a QR Code.

  • Upload your details like company logo
  • Select the number of trees’ links you need
  • Download a file with all your QR Codes (PNG format)


Create TreeCard

Design beautiful gift cards connected to trees.

  • Upload your details like company logo
  • Select or upload a TreeCard design (5*7 inch)
  • Download a PDF file with all your TreeCards


Manage Redeem Gift

Enable your customer or your employee to redeem trees.

  • Submit a landing page design for redemption
  • Send landing page URL (one URL) to customers
  • Trees can be redeemed by customers
