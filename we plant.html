Our Tree Planting Partners

EcoMatcher partners with carefully selected and vetted tree planting organisations from around the world.

Indonesia

NGO:Trees4Trees

Trees4Trees is an Indonesian non-profit that empowers local communities through partnership reforestation initiatives. By increasing the area of community planted and owned forests, livelihood assets are created, the negative effects of deforestation are reduced and the environment is renewed.

learn more










Philippines

NGO:Fostering Education & Environment for Development (FEED)

Fostering Education & Environment for Development (FEED) is a non-profit and non-governmental organization registered with the Philippine Securities and Exchange Commission. FEED supports sustainable education & tree planting, aiming to inclusively grow, preserve and protect Philippine biodiversity. Learn

learn more













Uganda

NGO:Tree Adoption Uganda (TAU)

Tree Adoption Uganda (TAU) is a youth led social enterprise that seeks to jointly address the challenges of youth unemployment and climate change. TAU has a vision of transforming environmental interventions into a means of creating sustainable socio-economic development, proactively alleviate poverty in communities and nurture growth.

learn more










Guatemala

NGO:The mission of the Alliance for International Reforestation Guatemala (AIR)

The mission of the Alliance for International Reforestation Guatemala (AIR), is to implement educational programs and agroforestry methods in Guatemala in order to protect water sources, prevent mudslides, reduce erosion and provide more nutritious crops while protecting the Earth. AIR also provides brick stoves with chimneys preventing lung diseases.

learn more










Thailand

NGO:Conserve Natural Forests (CNF)

Conserve Natural Forests (CNF) is an environmental non-profit foundation focused on forest restoration and wildlife conservation in the Mae Hong Son Province of Northern Thailand. Since 2014, CNF has have dedicated themselves to preserving and enriching ecosystem balance and function, restoring degraded landscapes, and reintroducing endangered species to their natural habitat.

learn more










nepal

NGO:New Growth

New Growth plants fruit, nut, and reforestation trees to aid communities and poor farmers in Simthali Nepal that deserve to have a sustainable future from economic, climate and environmental degradation.

learn more










India

NGO:SayTrees

Saytrees seek to save our planet by creating Forests , Water body conservation, Protection of Biodiversity, Helping farmers and to make citizens aware about the need of trees for our survival and protect natural resources for generations to come.

learn more










UAE

NGO:Emirates Marine Environmental Group

Emirates Marine Environmental Group (EMEG) is a non-profit organisation based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Established in 1996 under the patronage of Her Highness Sheikha Manal bint Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum and led by Major Ali Saqer Sultan Al Suwaidi.

learn more

Ecuador

NGO:ProAmazonia

PROAmazonía is a comprehensive program for forest conservation and sustainable production in Ecuador. PROAmazonía is an initiative of the United Nations (Development Program (UNDP)), the Ministry of Environment and Water, the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock, and is financed by the Green Climate Fund (GCF) and the Global Environment Facility (GEF).

learn more

NGO:Trees for Kenya

,

Trees for Kenya restores landscapes and improve livelihoods of communities through planting of trees. We also aim to fight climate change and enlighten communities on the impacts of climate change.

learn more










Jordan

NGO:Menaqua

Menaqua land restoration is a Netherlands based company that aims to restore degraded lands through reforestation and smart agroforestry tree planting initiatives, with a focus on the Middle East and North Africa (MENA). Our tree planting initiatives helps to restore the biodiversity value of degraded landscapes and enhances the socio-economic status of local communities who depend on this land.

learn more










Madagascar

NGO:Bony

Bôndy is a social enterprise led by young entrepreneurs who have the desire to reforest Madagascar, protect its biodiversity and help rural population thru its projects. To accomplish our mission, we propose agroforestry projects and mangroves restauration projects, as both can help us touch different communities and solve different environmental issues.

learn more










Haiti

NGO:The Haiti Tree Project

The Haiti Tree Project works with Haitian communities to help scale their pre-existing reforestation efforts. Through financial, educational, and technological assistance our goal is to create self-sustainability and food sovereignty while improving environmental conditions throughout Haiti.

learn more










Bhutan

NGO:My Bhutan

MyBhutan is a social enterprise co-founded by His Royal Highness Jigyel Ugyen Wangchuck and Matthew R. DeSantis with a deep-seated commitment to empowering local communities, conserving the environment and promoting sustainability in the Kingdom of Bhutan.

learn more










Malaysia

NGO:Regrow Borneo

Regrow Borneo aims to mitigate climate change and forest fragmentation by restoring degraded riverine and swamp forests in the Lower Kinabatangan Wildlife Sanctuary, in Sabah, Malaysian Borneo.

learn more










Qatar

NGO:Heenat Salma Farm

Heenat Salma is an eco-farm, located in Shahaniya, northwest of Doha. We are a multidisciplinary project dedicated to holistic methods in agriculture, architecture, and community development.

learn more
