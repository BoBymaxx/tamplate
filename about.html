Decade of Action

We live in the “decade of action”. Collective effort is required to address the biggest threat to humanity; the climate crisis. At EcoMatcher, we realize combating the climate crisis can also be a business opportunity for companies. Companies, however, need trustworthy partners that offer transparent sustainability solutions that meet client and employee demands. EcoMatcher helps companies achieve these sustainability goals.


Welcome to EcoMatcher

Through EcoMatcher, companies can plant trees and complete forests in a transparent way and use them in their businesses. For example, companies can use the EcoMatcher platform to gift trees they planted to customers or plant a whole forest and distribute trees to each of their employees. They can even gift trees as a reward for finalizing a survey. The possibilities are numerous. With EcoMatcher technology, every tree planted can also be virtually visited.

How do we do that

EcoMatcher partners with vetted foundations from around the world specialized in planting trees. Organizations that plant the right trees at the right time in the right place. Through technology, EcoMatcher knows everything about every tree, such as location, date of planting, species, etc. EcoMatcher offers a comprehensive digital platform enabling companies to integrate tree-planting into their businesses. A win-win for all, including Mother Nature

Advisory Board



Herman Wijffels

Herman Wijffels, Dutch economist and former CEO of Rabobank, is a widely recognized authority on sustainability, business and governance. He worked between 1981 and 1999 at the Rabobank, ultimately as CEO.From 1999 – 2006 he was chairman of the Dutch Social-Economic Council, an independent and influential advisory body of the government. From 2006 to 2008 Herman Wijffels was director and Dutch representative at the World Bank in Washington. Between 2006-2007 he lead the negotiations between the political parties to create a new Dutch government. From 2009 to 2016 Herman Wijffels was professor ‘Sustainability and Societal Change’ at Utrecht University.He chaired and led several innovative initiatives and committees, including the World Connectors and the Sustainable Finance Lab, to reform the banking sector. Some of those initiatives are carrying his name, such as the Herman Wijffels Innovation price
