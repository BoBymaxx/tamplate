About Mangrove

Mangrove planting is an important step in coastal conservation and preservation of important ecosystems. This helps protect coastlines from erosion, increases biodiversity, and provides habitat for a variety of marine and coastal species.

Technologies




EcoMatcher proudly develops all technologies offered in-house. The integrated technology platform consists of a trees’ data capturing app (TreeCorder), a blockchain and cloud-based system for viewing and managing trees’ data (TreeManager), and numerous ways to track trees on the web, EcoMatcher’s iOS/Android App and EcoMatcher’s App for Microsoft Teams.
